USER INTERFACE/USER EXPERIENCE DESIGNER

User Interface (UI) /User Experience (UX) Designer

Application deadline: Jan. 15, 2019

We are looking for an experienced and creative UI/UX Designer to join our team! As an UI-UX Designer , you will be responsible for delivering the best online user experience, which makes your role extremely important for our success and ensuring customer satisfaction and loyalty.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Collaborate with Marketing and Web teams to define the user experience vision for the website.
  • Align the web style with the corporate brand style and guidelines.
  • Conceptualize, illustrate, design and deliver intuitive and compelling design ideas using storyboards, mockups, prototypes, process flows and sitemaps that clearly supports customer needs and business objectives.
  • Drive research initiatives to acquire deep user understanding, evaluate usability and make data-driven decisions.
  • Advocate for the needs of our users throughout the development lifecycle in an Agile environment to guide the development team to produce the right solution.
  • Inspire design innovation by continuously researching and sharing design trends and championing the adoption of user-centered methodologies.
PERSONAL CHARACTERISTICS
  • Great communication skills
  • Logic and Reasoning
  • User empathy
  • Adaptability and responsiveness
  • Growth minded
  • Devoted to the project
SKILLS REQUIRED

Knowledge and experience in UX/UI design Good understanding of information architecture, wire-framing and visual communication. Confident user of Photoshop, Sketch, Adobe XD and Invision Perfect command of written English.

REQUIREMENT

Experience Levels: Intermediate (3 - 5 yrs)

Availability: Full Time

Minimum Qualification: Degree
